AWUN 1.7.0 identity, customization and search precision update
Release date: 2026-07-16

This build installs the new AWUN geometric mark across web, Windows, Android
and iOS. The visual system adds BLACK and WHITE themes plus compact, standard

obvious live streams, playlists and long-form mixes. Native apps can optionally
